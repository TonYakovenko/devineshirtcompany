Frequently Asked Questions

Can you create artwork for me?

Absolutely! Our rates for creating logos, and other artwork are $25 per hour. If you have your own artwork, it would need to meet our artwork guidelines to avoid additional charges. Please refer to the ‘Required Artwork Guidelines’ section of our site. You will be notified prior to being charged ANY extra fees with Devine Shirt Company.

Do you mix custom ink colours? Is there an added fee for this?

We offer custom pantone colour matching at no additional fee.

Am I able to print onto several different color shirts?

Yes, the shirts are already colored which means there is no extra cost to print on a blue shirt vs. a red shirt. However; be aware that you need to order at least 12 of each color.

It is necessary to use the same shirt size for my whole order?

Absolutely not, printing on various sizes is something we can easily accommodate. Generally XXL and larger shirts will cost more although youth sizes are available at no additional cost.

What if I order 200 shirts with multiple different designs on each?

No problem, we would merely separate them into different orders and then ship them out together to save money.

What shirts are normally used for the screen printing?

The choice is yours! High quality cotton t-shirts are generally used. We often print on brands such as Alstyle, Gildan, Bella and Canvas, Anvil, and many many more.

What are the shipping charges?

Local shipping within Winnipeg are 100% free, always. Outside of Winnipeg our shipping charges depend on the size of the package. We send all our packages Canada Post. We only charge you as much as it costs us to ship an item. Expedited shipping is available upon request.

Do you have a storefront?

No, all of our sales are done via Email and phone, and in many cases we can arrange to visit you at your convenience.

Can I supply my own garments?

We print on some supplied items if we cannot offer a suitable substitute. Because we cannot varify the quality and manufacturing processes of supplied items we do not offer any warranties should the print deteriorate after washing, please keep in mind that not all items are designed with with printing in mind.

What are the turnaround times?

In many cases 7 days from artwork and apparel approval to completion. Depending on how busy the shop is however it can be up to 2 weeks (14 days). Rush orders are accepted, but incur an additional fee. Please contact us if you have a time sensitive deadline that needs to be met, we can usually accomadate you.

Art Guidelines

If you want to supply your own ‘print ready’ artwork, please refer to these Art Guidelines for more info.

Artwork is considered “print ready” when it can be used for screen printing without the need for any adjustments that may possibly incur added fees or result in delays.
When submitting digital art files to Devine Shirt Company please refer to the below guidelines. If you have any questions email us at info@devineshirtcompany.ca.

Vector Art:
Vector artwork files must be saved up to Adobe Illustrator® CS6 (.ai, .eps, .pdf). All fonts must be converted to curves or outlines to prevent font substitutions. Include a proof of your design with color designations to confirm that no font substitutions have occurred. All objects must have at least a 1 pt line thickness to guarantee proper print quality. If the vector file contains any placed raster images, these images must meet or exceed our requirements for resolution when rendered at 100% of the final imprint size. Also, they must be submitted with the original vector file.

Programs:
We will accept files saved in Adobe Illustrator® CS6 providing that all text is converted to curves or outlines. We will also accept files saved in Adobe Photoshop®
CS6 at 100% of final print size with a resolution of at least 220ppi. Jpegs are most often useless if they are taken from the internet, but can be replicated for use in screen-printing at an fee of $25.

Fonts:
For vector art, text must be converted to paths, curves, or outlines. The text then becomes an object and can no longer be edited as text.

Line Thickness:
All design elements should contain a line thickness of at least 1pt. Failure to assign a 1 pt line thickness to all objects may compromise the final imprint quality.

Spot Colors:
Please specify color via PMS spot color designations to each object, text, or layer in your design. Due to hardware and software color calibration differences, this will allow the best color matching possible. We primarily use the Pantone Coated library. If you need more information about the Pantone Matching system please visit www.pantone.com

Proofs:
When creating designs for our customers, proofs are a way of assuring that we have set your type correctly and that all of the design elements are placed according to your requirements. A proof will be sent to your email address provided that can be approved with a simple reply. We will not start on any project until your proof has been approved.

Please email info@devineshirtcompany.ca with any questions you may have regarding digital art.
